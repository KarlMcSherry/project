Nightlife

Nightlife in Dublin City can be relatively expensive in comparison to other cities, with most drinks ranging from around €4 to €9. Temple Bar Square is in general the most expensive area, and prices will usually decline the further you are away.

Bars

  • O'Reilly's Bar - Tara Street

    O'Reilly's Bar offers pints of everything they have on tap for €4.20 all day, they also have a late bar on Monday, Wednesday, Friday and Saturday, with additional drink promotions.

  • The Porterhouse - Parliament Street

    The Porterhouse can be quite expensive for most drinks, however, for the beer connoisseur, they offer €5 pints of various micro-brewed ales, stouts and lagers.

  • Fibber Magees - Parnell Street

    Fibber Magees is a hard rock bar, their drink prices start at €3 and top off around €6.50, various drink promos are available and they have a late bar every night.

  • The Woolshed - Parnell Street

    The Woolshed is a sports bar that shows many types of sports, including football, American football, rugby, cricket and many more. Drink prices start at €3.50 and top off at €6.50, money can be saved by ordering pitchers of beer/cider/cocktails or bottles of wine.

Nightclubs

  • Hangar - St. Andrew's Lane

    Hangar is a nightclub that is open every night, specialising in electronic dance music. Hangar offers various promos every night, including cans of cider/beer for €3 and double spirits/mixer for €8. Price of entry can vary from €2 to €10

  • The Academy - Abbey Street

    The Academy is a nightclub that is primarily open on Friday and Saturday. Entry on a Friday costs €5, on Saturday, €10. Various promos on beer and spirits run both nights, with drinks starting at €3.50. Guestlist passes are offered weekly through the Vipsy app, though, in limited numbers on a first come first served basis.

  • The Palace - Camden Street

    The Palace is a nightclub which primarily plays chart music. The Palace is particularly cheap on a Friday, when they offer drinks starting at €2, guestlist passes are also usually offered through the Vipsy app.

  • Fibber Magees Downstairs - Parnell Street

    In addition to offering a bar upstairs, Fibber Magees offers a nightclub downstairs. This nightclub primarily hosts live heavy metal music, though, is known for hosting various events, from 80s themed club nights, to karaoke, to acoustic nights. Drink prices start at €3 and top off at €6.50, as with the bar upstairs. A cover charge usually ranging from €2 to €5 is also required.

Shows

  • Comedy Crunch - The Stag's Head - Dame Court

    Comedy Crunch is a stand-up club that runs every Monday, Tuesday and Sunday. Entry into Comedy Crunch is free, drink prices

  • The George - George's Street

    For people interested in drag shows, The George offers various types of shows hosted by drag queens every night, including karaoke and comedy. Entry is usually free before 10pm, or €10 after.

  • Trad Sessions - Mother Reilly's - Upper Rathmines Road

    Trad Sessions takes place each Monday, it is a show focused on the performance of traditional Irish music that is open for the audience to participate in, if they can play traditional Irish music. Entry is free.
